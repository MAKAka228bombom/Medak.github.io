About Viktor

Early Life

Born on August 28, 1952, Viktor Kurylenko grew up in a large Christian family, absorbing biblical truths from an early age. Despite facing challenges, including the loss of his eyesight at a young age, Viktor's faith remained strong.

Ministry

Viktor became a member of the Pentecostal Church at 18 and began preaching. He later became a youth leader and studied at the pastoral department of correspondence Bible courses in Moscow.

Work and Ministry

Radio Ministry

Viktor hosts the "Life" radio program, which is broadcast to CIS countries from Saipan Island.

Author

He has written several books, including "Our New Life" and is working on publishing a collection of his sermons.

Pastoral Care

In his church in Slavyansk, Viktor leads Bible studies, preaches, provides pastoral care, and heads a ministry for deliverance from demonic influence.
